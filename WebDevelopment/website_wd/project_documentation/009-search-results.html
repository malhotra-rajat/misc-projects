Search Results Page



When the user clocks on the search icon or presses Enter after typing a word in the search box on the right, he is taken to the search results page. On this page, the top 25 movie search results are shown with the following details:

  • Poster
  • Title
  • Rating
  • Year
  • Critics
  • Audience
